ArtBash 2023

My concept of ArtBash 2023 is revolution. I used the brick-like typeface to represent Core as the fundamental step for freshman students to start their art journey in SAIC. Just like the foundation is the most important part of architecture. In core, students experience ways of making arts that they never tried before. It’s a period of time for them to grow as a different social identity. Core gives students opportunities to break their thinking routine and force them to jump out of their comforzone, to be a contrarian. The color is a way of capturing the revolutionary energy of the program.
