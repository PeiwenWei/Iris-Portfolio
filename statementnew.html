Artist Statement

Iris Wei is a visual communication artist based in Chicago and Beijing, China. Visual image is a way for her to connect herself with this world. Graphic design, print media and photography are the methods she uses to reveal the contradiction in herself. Most of her inspiration comes from the radical emotions of a specific moment. She brings these emotions to the audience and asks who we really are. She enjoys the process of setting a theme, searching for information, and finally conveying the message to people in visually different ways. Before she starts a project, she would like to imagine people's thoughts and reactions after they see it. The interaction between people and her artwork is important for her. This interaction is spontaneous by the audience. It may be an abstract feeling or an exact idea.

For her, visual communication is a tool that helps the information become more impressive. Graphic design not only sends the message to the audience but also uses information to connect people to each other. From a business perspective, visual communication promotes the consumption of the brand. However, she found out people can receive emotions from the image they see.
